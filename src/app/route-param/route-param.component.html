RouteParamComponent works!

  • params: /myId

    • .snapshot.paramMap - {{idSnapshot}}

    • params: Observable<string> - {{id$ | async}}

  • optional param: a. /myId;optId=myOptId b. [routerLink]="['/routeparam', 'myId', { optId: 'myOptId' }]"

    • paramMap: Observable<string> {{idMap$ | async}}

  • query param: a. /myId?id=myId b. [routerLink]="['/routeparam', 'myId']" [queryParams]="{ id: 'myId' }"

    • queryParams: Observable<string> {{queryId$ | async}}

    • queryParamMap: Observable<string> [queryParams]="{ id: 'myId' }" - {{queryIdMap$ | async}}

  • Others:

    • data: Observable<Data> - {{data$ | async}}

    • url: Observable<UrlSegment[]> - {{url$ | async}}
